👋 Welcome — start here

This is your complete, self-paced curriculum to become an expert in Compressed Stabilized Earth Block (CSEB) manufacturing and the business of building with earth, tailored for launching in Ghana.

Take 2 minutes to read this welcome guide before you start — it will save you hours of confusion later.

1

The six navigation tabs

At the top you'll see six buttons:

  • 📚 Modules — the main training (13 modules)
  • 🧮 Calculators — three practical tools for soil mixing, strength testing, and unit economics
  • 📖 Glossary — searchable definitions of 58 technical terms
  • 🎴 Flashcards — 120 cards with spaced repetition to refresh your memory anytime
  • 📕 Library — 25 highly-recommended books across CSEB technology, faith and business, leadership, and African entrepreneurship
  • 📅 Study Planner — printable week-by-week plan with your start date

You can switch between them anytime without losing progress.

2

How the modules work

Each module contains:

  • Teaching content with diagrams, tables, and examples
  • Video links (open in new tabs to YouTube)
  • Resource links to PDFs and reference sites
  • A hands-on assignment at the end
  • A quiz requiring 80% to mark the module complete

You can retake quizzes as many times as needed.

3

The recommended path

  1. Start with Module 0: Preparation & Resources (1 hour) — it tells you about formal training options (Hydraform, Auroville, Makiga)
  2. Work through Modules 1-12 in order — they build on each other
  3. Take the Final Certification Exam (30 questions, 80% to pass) once all core modules are done
  4. Pursue formal hands-on training when ready
4

How long it takes

At 5 hours per week, the full training takes approximately 12 months. Total content: ~250 hours.

Need a different pace? Open the 📅 Study Planner tab — you can adjust your weekly hours and see exact dates.

5

Your progress saves automatically — per profile

This app supports multiple profiles on the same device. Each profile keeps its own progress, quiz scores, and flashcard ratings.

  • Click your name/avatar in the top right to switch profiles or create new ones
  • Progress is per profile per browser per device — laptop progress doesn't appear on your phone
  • Clearing your browser data erases all profiles
  • The app works completely offline once loaded (videos and external links still need internet)
6

Tips for getting the most out of it

  • Take notes in a real notebook — handwritten notes lock in learning
  • Watch the videos — they show what text can't
  • Do the assignments — they turn knowledge into skill
  • Use the calculators with your real Ghana numbers
  • Don't skip the prep module — choosing the right formal training is the highest-impact decision you'll make

🧮 Interactive Calculators

Three practical tools to use during your training and once you're in production. All calculations run locally in your browser — no data is sent anywhere.

Soil Recipe Calculator

Tells you how much soil, cement, and water to mix for your target number of blocks. Based on standard CSEB practice (Auroville Earth Institute methodology).

Compression Strength Calculator

Converts your compression test results (force at failure + block dimensions) into compressive strength in MPa. Use this every time you test a block.

Unit Economics Modeler

Estimates your cost per block, profit per block, and monthly profit in Ghana cedis. Update with your real local prices for accurate results.
